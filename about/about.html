Welcome from the Director

Welcome! Thank you for visiting our website and taking a closer look at Rocky Hill School. For more than 80 years Rocky Hill has been providing a challenging, college preparatory education with a unique focus on the individual student, academic excellence, and experiential learning. The combination of Harkness discussion-based learning and small classes puts students at the center of every class. At the table, students think critically, express their ideas, work collaboratively, and solve problems. All of this prepares them for success in college and beyond. Our admission process is personalized and designed to educate families about the unique advantages of the Rocky Hill School experience. Be assured we will help you every step of the way. I invite you to plan a visit to our 84-acre waterfront campus and see our remarkable school for yourself. Tours of the campus can be scheduled at your convenience during the week. Please contact Sarah Taylor, Admission Assistant, at 401-884-9070 ext. 107 to make an appointment.
